About

Kizil is an app mainly designed for neurodivergent individuals to help them read faster and easier. Kizil creates a better reading experience with custom preferences like background, text colors and font changes. You can change the font, bigger the font, add spaces between lines, increase or decrease line height and many more things. If you are having troubles trying to figure out the app, you can contact us.
